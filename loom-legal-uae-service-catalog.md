# loom-legal UAE Government Service Catalog (B2C Self-Service Tier)

**Target customer:** Individuals in the UAE who cannot afford lawyers (AED 29–149 / case).  
**Product mechanic:** AI scrapes the form → asks plain-language questions → pre-fills → generates risk score → submits online (or produces PDF + step-by-step guide if auto-submission is blocked).  
**Schema:** `id | jurisdiction | portal_url | service_name_en | service_name_ar | category | fee_aed | sla_days | required_inputs | typical_outcome | success_rate | risks | related_articles`

## Three Critical Risks & Mitigations

### 1. Regulatory Risk — Unauthorized Practice of Law (UPL)
- **The Risk:** UAE Federal Law No. 23 of 1991 (as amended) restricts court representation and "legal advice" to licensed advocates registered with the MOJ. If loom-legal is perceived as giving legal advice or representing clients, the founders could face criminal penalties and platform shutdown.
- **Mitigation:**
  - **Hard disclaimer on every artifact:** *"loom-legal is a document-preparation and form-filling assistant. We are not a law firm. Nothing herein constitutes legal advice."*
  - **Never sign pleadings or appear:** The system pre-fills; the customer clicks "submit" with their own UAE Pass.
  - **Escalation gate:** Any case involving felonies, deportation risk, >AED 500k, or active detention is automatically blocked from self-service and routed to a licensed partner law firm (revenue-share referral).
  - **Regulatory wrapper:** Register the operating entity as a "smart government services" or typing-center licensee in Dubai (DED license for IT/document services), not as a legal consultancy.

### 2. Ethical Risk — Wrong Advice → Irreversible Harm
- **The Risk:** An AI-generated error (e.g., missing the 30-day reconsideration deadline, miscalculating a travel-ban risk, or filing in the wrong jurisdiction) could cause a customer to lose a case, miss a flight, or face deportation. For B2C individuals who cannot afford lawyers, this harm is existential.
- **Mitigation:**
  - **Date-deadline guardrails:** Hard-coded calculators with **3-day safety buffers** for all statutory deadlines (reconsideration, appeal, MOHRE referral, labor court filing). System flashes red if user is within buffer.
  - **Human-in-the-loop for high-stakes submissions:** Services priced >AED 99 require a loom-legal "case reviewer" (non-lawyer trained operator) to verify field accuracy before the customer submits.
  - **Precedent corpus = risk score only:** The AI uses court precedent to generate a *probability* (e.g., "73% favorable"), never a guarantee. Output explicitly lists the failure modes (see catalog column).
  - **Mandatory lawyer referral ladder:** If the risk score is <40% or the case type is in the "failure modes" escalation list, the customer cannot pay for submission; they receive a PDF guide + lawyer match instead.

### 3. Operational Risk — Portal Changes, Scraper Breakage & Form Updates
- **The Risk:** UAE government portals (especially UAE Pass integrations) update frequently without warning. A scraper that worked yesterday can fail today, causing customer payments to fail, data to be lost, or incorrect forms to be submitted.
- **Mitigation:**
  - **Health-check monitoring:** Run automated end-to-end tests on all 40 portal forms every **4 hours**. Alert on CSS-selector mismatches, CAPTCHA changes, or new mandatory fields.
  - **Instant fallback workflow:** If scraper confidence <95%, the system immediately switches to **PDF + step-by-step guide** mode. The customer receives a pre-filled PDF with every field mapped to the portal screenshot; they copy-paste and submit manually. No refund needed because value is still delivered.
  - **API-first where possible:** Prioritize official APIs over scraping: **Dubai REST** (DLD), **GDRFA APIs**, **MOHRE inquiry APIs**, **FTA EmaraTax** (if taxpayer consents). APIs are more stable and legally safer than headless browsing.
  - **Version-locked form cache:** Store the last-known-good form HTML/JSON for every service. If the live site changes, the cached version is used to generate the fallback PDF while engineers fix the scraper.
