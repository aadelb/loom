--- research_steganography_covert_channel_detector ---
The Steganography & Covert Channel Detector addresses the highly sophisticated methods threat actors use to exfiltrate data, communicate command-and-control (C2) instructions, or distribute malicious payloads within seemingly benign files. Steganography is the practice of concealing a file, message, image, or video within another file. For AI safety research, it is critical to determine if generative AI models (like image generators or code assistants) are being manipulated.
