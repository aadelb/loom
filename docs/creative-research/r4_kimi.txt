1. ShadowSentinel  
   Core: dark-web beacon triangulation × AI-researcher psychometrics × career-momentum vectors × code-reuse forensics.  
   Novel capability: In real time it spots a previously invisible “insider-risk trajectory” inside the global AI-Safety talent pool.  
   • Dark-web: detects the first appearance of a never-before-seen jailbreak prompt, leaked model weights, or unpublished exploit in closed-forum paste dumps.  
   • Psychological profiling: infers the latent risk tolerance, need-for-cognition, and perfectionism scores of every researcher who has ever touched the leaky model’s code base.  
   • Career analytics: tracks citation velocity, funding pressure, and peer-influence centrality of those same researchers.  
   • Security scanning: maps which private repositories, compute grants, or infra keys each researcher still has access to.  
   Output: a Risk-Vector Graph that flags the specific individual who—given their psychological profile, career stress, and remaining access—is probabilistically most likely to weaponize the newly observed exploit before anyone else even knows it exists. Traditional security scans or psych tests alone can never reveal this convergence.
