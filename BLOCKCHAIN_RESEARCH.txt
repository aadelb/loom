RECOMMENDED NEW LOOM TOOLS
==============================================================================

Based on research, these 7 new tools should be implemented:

1. research_crypto_risk_score
   Based on: cryptowallet_risk_scoring
   Purpose: Wallet reputation and financial risk assessment
   Inputs: wallet_address, blockchain (eth/btc/sol)
   Outputs: risk_score, risk_factors, recommendation
   Effort: LOW | Timeline: Week 1

2. research_ethereum_tx_decode
   Based on: ethtx_ce
   Purpose: Decode and semantically analyze Ethereum transactions
   Inputs: transaction_hash, block
   Outputs: function_calls, parameters, transfers, decoded_data
   Effort: LOW | Timeline: Week 1

3. research_transaction_graph
   Based on: pytorch_geometric
   Purpose: Build GNN-based transaction network analysis
   Inputs: address_list, time_range, depth
   Outputs: graph_data, anomalies, network_metrics
   Effort: MEDIUM | Timeline: Week 2-3

4. research_crypto_aml_check
   Based on: ethguardian-AI + Money_Laundering_Detection
   Purpose: Detect money laundering patterns in transactions
   Inputs: address, transaction_hash, time_window
   Outputs: aml_risk_score, pattern_type, confidence, algorithm_used
   Effort: MEDIUM | Timeline: Week 2-3

5. research_defi_security_audit
   Based on: plamen + SmartAuditFlow
   Purpose: Automated smart contract vulnerability detection
   Inputs: contract_code, contract_address, blockchain
   Outputs: vulnerabilities, severity, remediation, explanation
   Effort: MEDIUM | Timeline: Week 3-4

6. research_token_auditor
   Based on: Onchain-Security-Suite
   Purpose: Analyze ERC-20/721 tokens for fraud/rug-pull risk
   Inputs: token_address, blockchain
   Outputs: audit_score, red_flags, deployer_reputation, transfer_analysis
   Effort: MEDIUM | Timeline: Week 3-4

7. research_address_clustering
   Based on: bitcoin-address-clustering
   Purpose: Identify and cluster related blockchain addresses
   Inputs: seed_address, blockchain, clustering_heuristics
   Outputs: cluster_members, relationship_type, confidence_scores
   Effort: MEDIUM | Timeline: Week 2-3
